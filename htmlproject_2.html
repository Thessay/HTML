Thess Restaurant's Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Bruschetta al Pomodoro

    Toasted baguette slices topped with diced tomatoes, garlic, fresh basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.

  • Prosciutto e Melone

    Thin slices of prosciutto wrapped around fresh cantaloupe or honeydew melon.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Crostini with Ricotta & Honey

    Crispy bread topped with creamy ricotta, a drizzle of honey, and sometimes crushed walnuts.

  • Greek Salad Skewers

    Skewered cubes of feta, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and olives drizzled with olive oil.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Stuffed Cherry Tomatoes

    Hollowed-out cherry tomatoes filled with herbed goat cheese or cream cheese.

  • Antipasto Platter Bites

    Small skewers of olives, marinated artichokes, salami, and cheese.
